Our Services

  • ESTATE PLANNING

    We prepare legal documents necessary for a complete and effective estate plan. Estate planning takes time and thought, but does not need to be complicated. An effective estate plan often includes a Will, Power of Attorney, and Health Care Directive.

  • LETTERS PROBATE

    We help executors by preparing and submitting the application for letters probate. We are familiar with the proper procedure and forms required for these applications. There is so much to do as an executor, so let us help you with this part.

  • BESTATE ADMINISTRATION

    We work with executors and administrators by providing advice and guidance on how to properly settle an estate. We take the time to explain what tasks need to be completed by an individual administering an estate. We can also prepare the necessary legal documents if a court application is required. ​
